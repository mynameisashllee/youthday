# Level 2 Youth Day Design Sync

## What is Level 2 doing

- The entrance level where people first arrive
- Information-heavy area with registration and orientation
- Greenish theme for internal spaces, youth day colors for entrance
- Multiple touchpoints and activity zones throughout the space

### Commune

- Registration area with new normal colors (loud and vibrant)
- Information about what people can expect on each level
- Distribution point for:
  - Tote bags with custom embroidery patches
  - Custom badges/pins for quest line activities
  - Wristbands for food vendor discounts (10% off with wristband)
- Very information-heavy visually - needs toning down from current mock-ups

### Beside commune

- Collaborative MRT map board for interaction
- Questions about where people stay in Singapore
- Visitors place stickers/post-it notes on map locations
- Custom sticker sheets possible if budget allows
- Big bold slogan needed (content TBD)
- Blue tack or extra adhesive needed if using post-it notes

### Ramp to entrance

- Streamers in theme colors along railings
- Kite-shaped paper attached to streamers as directional signage
- Small buckets replacing balloons with youth day message cards
- Cards designed by team - same design, different positive messages
- Possible origami element for interactive folding activity
- Simple open/fold mechanism needed since people walking

### Entrance

- Big banner across overhead area
- Large decorative boxes at entrance (similar to level reference image)
- Info posters and vibe messages rather than overly informative content
- Need to design different sides of entrance boxes

### Info booth

- General inquiries about the event
- Quest line information and directions
- Information about participating communities/organizations
- Volunteer staffing planned for the area
- Chalk area for co-creating (location to be confirmed)

### Camping ground

- Stage setup with PPTK partnership
- Open stage for community announcements
- Prop story linking booth activity:
  - Give participants props (sunglasses, funny hats)
  - Create stories linking all props together
  - Award badge/pin for participation
- Poster needed for prop booth
- Various programs possible: singing, stand-up comedy, game shows
- Food vendors with 10% discount for wristband holders
- Promotional posters needed for vendor discount program

### Mirror space

- Career ladder concept adapted for “get to know each other”
- Area cordoned off with big poster
- Prompt cards for conversation starters
- Alternative: QR code linking to prompt generator website
- Lower priority item

### Brick wall

- Community partners designing their own materials
- Filler posters needed if partners don’t provide enough content
- Request partners to create additional posters if possible

### Dog

- Secondary registration point (smaller version of main registration)
- Drop-off point from outside entrance
- Registration booth setup similar to main commune area

### Plaza

- Lighting system with programmable colors:
  - 8 wash lights
  - 24 battery pars
  - 8 beams
  - 2 large spotlights
- Colors changeable throughout the day
- Fairy lights possible around plaza perimeter and railings
- People positioned on every balcony level during events
- Bundle cost: $4.5k including audio equipment

### Design considerations

- Budget: $6-8k per level
- Custom pins: $4-5 per piece, 1000+ quantity possible
- Longer lead times needed for custom merchandise
- Printing limited to A3 size in-house
- External printing needed for banners
- Balance needed between vibrant youth day theme and overwhelming visuals
- Eliminate balloons from current designs
- Reduce text-heavy elements in graphics
