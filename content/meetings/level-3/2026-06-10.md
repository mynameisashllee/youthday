# Level 3 Youth Day Design Sync

## Overview

Level 3 is the **social floor** — a NYC loft living room vibe centred on belonging and outward connection. Distinct from Level 4's rest-and-reflection tone, this floor is about communal energy: low tables, bean bags, floor-level sofas, and shared experiences.

---

  - Beans and Beets partnership in discussion with Matteo — complicated by profit/nonprofit structure
  - DIY fallback: multiple high tables arranged in a circle
  - Pillar placement may prevent a true 360° layout

- Large open space — activity concept still being finalised. Options:

  2. Board games area

## Deliverables

**Signage**
- New York street-style directional signs: *"Coffee here"*, *"Vinyl over there"*, *"Board games over there"*
- Corkboard for collaborative messages and doodles

**Merch & collectibles**
- **Enamel pins** — one pin per level completed; fits into printed logo squares (NYT tiles game style); Level 3 pin uses a Persian rug design
- **Mini vinyl keychains** — participants draw their favourite album cover and keep the keychain; supplier still needs to be sourced
- **Custom disco ball coin pouch** — potential alternative or addition to the extra pins

## Design Notes

**Aesthetic references**
- NYC loft living room: warm, layered, lived-in
- Low and grounded furniture throughout — bean bags, floor sofas, low tables
- Persian rug motif carried into the Level 3 pin design

**Pin system**
- Each level has its own pin; visitors collect one per level completed
- Pins slot into a printed card with logo squares, NYT tiles-style
- Level 3 design: Persian rug aesthetic
