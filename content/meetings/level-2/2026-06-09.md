# Level 2 Youth Day Design Sync

## Overview

Level 2 is the entrance floor — where people first arrive, register, and get oriented. The design theme is **greenish for internal spaces** and **Youth Day colors (loud, vibrant)** for the entrance-facing areas. The current mock-ups are visually overwhelming and need to be toned down.

---

## Spaces

### Commune (Registration)

- Main registration area with vibrant Youth Day colors
- Distributes tote bags, custom badges/pins, and food-vendor wristbands (10% off)
- Displays what visitors can expect on each level
- Current designs are text-heavy — reduce visual clutter

### Beside Commune

- Collaborative MRT map wall: visitors place stickers or post-it notes showing where they live in Singapore
- Custom sticker sheets possible if budget allows (blue tack or extra adhesive needed for post-its)
- Needs a large, bold slogan — content TBD

### Ramp to Entrance

- Streamers in theme colors along the railings
- Kite-shaped paper attached to streamers as directional signage
- Small buckets (replacing balloons) holding Youth Day message cards
- Cards share the same design with different positive messages — team designs these
- Possible origami element for an interactive folding activity (needs to be simple enough for people walking)

### Entrance

- Large overhead banner
- Decorative boxes at entrance (ref. Level 2 image) — different sides need distinct graphic treatments
- Info posters and vibe messaging; avoid heavy informational content here

### Info Booth

- Handles general event inquiries, quest line directions, and info on participating communities/orgs
- Volunteer-staffed
- Chalk co-creation area nearby (location TBC)

### Camping Ground

- Stage setup with PPTK partnership; open stage for community announcements
- **Prop story booth**: give participants props (sunglasses, hats), participants create a story linking all props together, award a badge/pin for participation — needs a poster
- Various programs: singing, stand-up comedy, game shows
- Food vendors on-site; wristband holders get 10% off — needs a promo poster

### Mirror Space

- Adapted career-ladder concept reframed as a get-to-know-each-other activity
- Cordoned-off area with a large poster and prompt cards for conversation starters
- Alternative: QR code linking to a prompt-generator website
- Lower priority

### Brick Wall

- Community partners create their own materials for this wall
- Filler posters needed if partners don't provide enough content
- Ask partners to contribute additional pieces if possible

### Secondary Registration (DOG)

- Smaller version of the main Commune setup
- Drop-off point from the outside entrance

### Plaza

- Programmable lighting: 8 wash lights, 24 battery pars, 8 beams, 2 large spotlights — colors changeable throughout the day
- Fairy lights possible around the plaza perimeter and railings
- People positioned on every balcony level during events
- Bundle cost: ~$4.5k including audio
