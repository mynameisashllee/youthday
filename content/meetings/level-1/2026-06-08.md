# Level 1 Youth Day Design Sync

## Overview

Level 1's concept is **neo:normal** — a neon-lit, holographic world that invites people to revisit childhood nostalgia through mini golf, glow-in-the-dark elements, and DJ music. The intent is playful and exploratory, not pressurising. The aesthetic reference is **Wong Kar-wai**: cinematic neon done with restraint, not tacky club energy.

The floor is workshop-heavy but partner payments are unresolved and most activities are still unconfirmed. Nothing is locked in yet.

## Design Notes

**Aesthetic direction**
- Wong Kar-wai cinematic neon — warm, layered, intentional
- Holographic materials, black lights, purple ambient lighting
- Minimalist execution to avoid cheapness; not a club, not a fairground

**Key constraint**
- Tenant cooperation is delicate — the space must look like a curated event, not a takeover that threatens their brand

**Merch and custom items**
